###### ScrollComponent.scrollToPoint(object, boolean, object) Scroll to a specific point, optionally animating. Takes three arguments: a point (x and y), animate (true or false) and animationOptions. 
scroll = new ScrollComponent()

# Scroll content to x:300, y:100
scroll.scrollToPoint({x:200, y:100}, true, {curve:"ease"})

# Scroll very slowly, with a different notation
scroll.scrollToPoint(
	{x:200, y:100}, 
	animate = true,
	animationOptions = {curve:"ease", time:10}
)

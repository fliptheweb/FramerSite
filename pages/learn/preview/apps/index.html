{% endblock sidebar %} {% block body %} {% filter pygmentify %} {% filter markdown %} ### Apps Get the best browsing experience on mobile devices with applicartions designed especially for previewing prototypes with Framer. ---
Framer for Android

The offical Framer for Android app, with a full-screen chromeless browser and automatic detection of all open projects.

Full-screen browsing

A chromeless browser that hides all controls for an immersive experience.

Customizable gestures

Swipe up or shake to reveal the controls and swipe left and right between projects.

Connect with Studio

Instantly detects all of your open projects, and those of others on your network.

---
Frameless for iOS

An iOS8 browser made for designers and developers who need an easy way to preview prototypes on iOS devices.

Transparent UI

No browser chrome and no status bar. Gestures control your browsing history.

Customizable gestures

Swipe up, tap with three fingers or shake the device to reveal the keyboard.

Connect with Studio

Automatically connect to Framer Studio and see all of your opened projects.

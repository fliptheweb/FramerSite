{% endblock sidebar %} {% block body %} {% filter pygmentify %} {% filter markdown %} ### Preview Prototypes There are 2 apps that help you preview your prototypes, on iOS and Android devices. You can also preview prototypes directly in a WebKit browser. This feature is called Mirror. Connect your device to the same Wi-Fi network as your Mac. Select your project, and preview in full-screen for distraction-free prototyping.
Framer for Android

The official Framer for Android app, with a full-screen chrome-less browser and automatic detection of all open projects.

Download
  • A chrome-less browser that hides all controls for an immersive experience.
  • Swipe up or shake to reveal the controls and swipe left and right between projects.
  • Instantly detects all of your open projects, and those of others on your network.
Frameless for iOS

An iOS8 browser made for designers and developers who need an easy way to preview prototypes on iOS devices.

Download
  • No browser chrome and no status bar. Gestures control your browsing history.
  • Swipe up, tap with three fingers or shake the device to reveal the keyboard.
  • Automatically connect to Framer Studio and see all of your opened projects.
  • Visit the Frameless site and follow Jay Stakelon on Twitter.

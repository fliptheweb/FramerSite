{% endblock sidebar %} {% block body %} {% filter pygmentify %} {% filter markdown %} ### Mirror To start previewing your prototypes, click the **"Mirror"** icon in the toolbar. You can open the link presented in the popover menu on your mobile device. The share button can be used to quickly send yourself or others the link via message or email.
##### Browse Prototypes To see all of your active documents within a web browser, simply remove your project name from the URL to get to the root of the server.
On iOS, you can add your project to your Homescreen by tapping the Share button and then "Add to Home Screen". This allows you to view your prototypes without browser chrome. ---

{% endblock sidebar %} {% block body %} {% filter pygmentify %} {% filter markdown %} ### Preview on Mobile If you've sent yourself the link to your prototype via email or text, opening it on your mobile device automatically launches the browser with your prototype. To see all of your active documents, simply remove your project name from the URL to get to the root of the server.

**On iOS**, you can add your project to your Homescreen by tapping the Share button and then **"Add to Home Screen"**. This allows you to view your prototypes without browser chrome. --- ##### Notes - The **inital-scale** meta tag is set to 0.5 by default, for creating prototypes in @2x.
You can change this when working in @1x. - To save your prototype for offline (cached) viewing, add /offline.html to the URL.
Note that his doesn't work well with auto refreshing. ---

Educational Discount

We offer 50% discount to all students and teachers. To apply, please send us a copy of your enrollment in, or connection to, the school that you attend. Contact us, if you have additional educational licensing needs.

License

The Framer license is on a per user base. If you have multiple machines (for example one at home and one at the office) you are free to use Framer on both. If you use your license on more machines than what we consider fair use, we might disable your license.
